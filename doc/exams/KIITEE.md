# KIITEE (KIIT Entrance Examination)

## Official Links & Dates (2025)
- **Official Website:** [https://kiitee.kiit.ac.in/](https://kiitee.kiit.ac.in/)
- **Registration Start:** November 2024
- **Registration End:** March 2025 (Phase 1)
- **Exam Dates:** March 2025 (Phase 1)

## Overview
KIITEE is conducted by Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology for admission to its various courses. There is **No Application Fee**.

## Data Requirements

### 1. Personal Details
- Candidate's Name (First, Middle, Last).
- Father's Name.
- Mother's Name.
- Date of Birth.
- Gender.
- Nationality.
- Category (General/SC/ST/OBC/Physically Challenged).

### 2. Contact Details
- Address for Communication.
- Mobile Number.
- Email Address.

### 3. Academic Details
- **Qualifying Exam:** 10+2.
- **Status:** Passed / Appearing.
- **Board/University.**
- **Roll Number.**
- **Year of Passing.**
- **Percentage of Marks.**

### 4. Course & Center
- **Course Applying For:** B.Tech (4 Years).
- **Exam Center Preferences:** Select 2 choices.

### 5. Documents to Upload
| Document | Format | Size Min | Size Max |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

## Special Notes
- **Free Application:** A key differentiator is that applying is free of cost.
