# SRMJEEE (SRM Joint Engineering Entrance Examination)

## Official Links & Dates (2025)
- **Official Website:** [https://applications.srmist.edu.in/btech](https://applications.srmist.edu.in/btech)
- **Registration Start:** November 2024
- **Registration End:** 
  - **Phase 1:** April 2025
  - **Phase 2:** June 2025
- **Exam Dates:**
  - **Phase 1:** April 2025
  - **Phase 2:** June 2025

## Overview
SRMJEEE is the common entrance exam for B.Tech admissions across SRM Institute of Science and Technology (SRMIST) campuses.

## Data Requirements

### 1. Registration
- Name.
- Email ID.
- Mobile Number.
- State & City.
- Create Password.

### 2. Application Form
- **Preferences:** Course Preference (B.Tech), Campus Preferences.
- **Personal Details:** Father's Name, Mother's Name, DOB, Gender, Nationality, Community.
- **Academic Details:**
  - Class 10 Marks & Details.
  - Class 12 Marks & Details (Status: Appearing/Passed).
  - Institute Name, Board.
- **Address:** Correspondence & Permanent.

### 3. Documents to Upload
| Document | Format | Size Min | Size Max |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Photograph** | JPG/JPEG | 10 KB | 200 KB |
| **Signature** | JPG/JPEG | 10 KB | 200 KB |

## Special Notes
- **Remote Proctoring:** SRMJEEE is often conducted as a Remote Proctored Online Exam (RPOM), meaning students take it from home. The application might ask for system compatibility checks (Webcam/Mic availability).
