# AP EAPCET (Andhra Pradesh Engineering, Agriculture and Pharmacy Common Entrance Test)

## Official Links & Dates (2026)
- **Official Website:** [https://cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/EAPCET/](https://cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/EAPCET/)
- **Registration Status:** **NOT YET OPEN**
- **Expected Start:** March 2026
- **Exam Dates:** May 12 - 20, 2026 (Tentative)

## Overview
Conducted by JNTU Kakinada on behalf of APSCHE for admission into professional courses in Andhra Pradesh.

## Data Requirements

### 1. Registration
- Qualifying Exam Hall Ticket Number (Intermediate/10+2).
- Candidate's Name.
- Date of Birth.
- Mobile Number.
- Alternate Mobile Number.
- Email ID.
- Stream: Engineering / Agriculture & Pharmacy.

### 2. Candidate Details
- **Father's Name.**
- **Mother's Name.**
- **Aadhar Card Number.**
- **Ration Card Number:** (Optional/Required for fee reimbursement check).
- **Annual Income of Parents:** (<1 Lakh / 1-2.5 Lakh / >8 Lakh).
- **Caste Category:** OC / BC-A/B/C/D/E / SC / ST.
  - Caste Certificate Number (MeeSeva Number).
- **Special Categories:** NCC / CAP / Sports / PH / Anglo-Indian.
- **Local Area Status:** AU (Andhra University) / SVU (Sri Venkateswara Univ) / OU (Osmania) / Non-Local.

### 3. Academic Details
- **SSC (Class 10):** Hall Ticket No, Passing Year.
- **Intermediate (Class 12):** College Name, Place of Study (District).
- **Medium of Instruction:** English / Telugu / Urdu.

### 4. Test Centre Preference
- Select Primary District and Secondary District.
- Select 3 preferred test center locations.

### 5. Documents to Upload
| Document | Format | Size Min | Size Max |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Photograph** | JPG/JPEG | 10 KB | 30 KB |
| **Signature** | JPG/JPEG | 10 KB | 15 KB |

## Special Notes
- **Photo/Sign Size:** Note the extremely small file size limits (Max 30KB for photo, 15KB for sign).
- **Regional Proof:** The "Local Area" logic determines 85% seat reservation, so correct selection based on study history (Class 6-12) is vital.
