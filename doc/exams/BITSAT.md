# BITSAT (Birla Institute of Technology and Science Admission Test)

## Official Links & Dates (2026)
- **Official Website:** [https://admissions.bits-pilani.ac.in/](https://admissions.bits-pilani.ac.in/)
- **Registration Status:** **OPEN**
- **Registration Start:** December 15, 2025
- **Registration End:** March 16, 2026 (Session 1 & 2)
- **Exam Dates:**
  - **Session 1:** April 15 - 17, 2026
  - **Session 2:** May 24 - 26, 2026

## Overview
BITSAT is the computer-based online test for admissions to Integrated First Degree Programmes of BITS Pilani Campuses in Pilani, Goa, and Hyderabad.

## Data Requirements

### 1. Registration
- Name.
- Gender.
- Date of Birth.
- Email ID.
- Mobile Number.

### 2. Personal Details
- Father's Name.
- Mother's Name.
- Nationality.
- Address for Correspondence.
- City, Pin Code, State.
- Landline Number (Optional).

### 3. Class 12 Examination Details
- Name of Institution (Class 12).
- Name of Examination Board.
- Year of Passing/Appearing.
- **Subject Selection:**
  - Mathematics (for B.E.).
  - Biology (for B.Pharm).

### 4. Exam Center Preference
- Choice of 3 Exam Centers (from the provided list).
- Option to select **Dubai** as a center (International fees apply).

### 5. Documents to Upload
| Document | Format | Size Min | Size Max |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Photograph** | JPG/JPEG | 50 KB | 100 KB |
| **Signature** | JPG/JPEG | 10 KB | 50 KB |

## Special Notes
- **Photo Size:** Note the minimum size is 50 KB, which is larger than many other exams (which start at 10 KB). This often causes issues for applicants reusing compressed files.
- **Marks Input:** During later stages (or counseling), extremely detailed input of Class 12 marks (Physics, Chemistry, Maths, English, Total) is required to verify the "75% aggregate" rule.
