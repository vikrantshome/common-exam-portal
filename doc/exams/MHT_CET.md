# MHT CET (Maharashtra Health and Technical Common Entrance Test)

## Official Links & Dates (2026)
- **Official Website:** [https://cetcell.mahacet.org/](https://cetcell.mahacet.org/)
- **Registration Status:** **OPEN**
- **Registration Start:** January 10, 2026
- **Registration End:** February 12, 2026
- **Exam Dates:**
  - **PCM Group:** April 11 - 19, 2026
  - **PCB Group:** April 21 - 26, 2026

## Overview
MHT CET is a state-level entrance exam conducted by the State Common Entrance Test Cell, Maharashtra, for admission to Engineering and Pharmacy courses in Maharashtra.

## Data Requirements

### 1. Personal Details
- **Candidate Name:** Full name.
- **Father's Name:** First name only usually required separately.
- **Mother's Name:** First name only.
- **Gender:** Male/Female/Transgender.
- **Date of Birth:** DD/MM/YYYY.
- **Religion:** Hindu, Muslim, Christian, etc.
- **Region:** Rural/Urban.
- **Mother Tongue:** Marathi, Hindi, English, etc.
- **Annual Family Income:** Select range.
- **Nationality:** Indian.

### 2. Domicile & Category (CRITICAL)
- **Domicile:** Maharashtra / Other than Maharashtra (OMS).
- **Category:** Open / SC / ST / DT/VJ / NT-1 / NT-2 / NT-3 / OBC / SBC.
- **Caste Name:** Select from list (if reserved).
- **Non-Creamy Layer (NCL):** Required for OBC, SBC, VJ/DT, NT categories (Valid till March 31 of admission year).
- **PwD:** Yes/No (Type of disability).
- **EWS:** Yes/No.

### 3. Contact Details
- **Address:** House No, Street, City/Village, Taluka, District, State, PIN Code.
- **Mobile Number:** Valid Indian number.
- **Email ID:** Valid email.

### 4. Academic Details
- **SSC (Class 10):**
  - Board Name (Maharashtra / CBSE / ICSE / IB / Others).
  - Year of Passing.
  - Percentage/Marks obtained.
  - State of Schooling.
- **HSC (Class 12):**
  - Board Name.
  - Status: Appeared / Passed.
  - Place of Schooling: Maharashtra / Outside Maharashtra.

### 5. Exam Details
- **Group:** PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Maths) / PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology) / Both.
- **Language of Question Paper:** English / Marathi / Urdu.
- **Exam Center Preferences:** Select District preferences (Priority 1, 2, 3, 4).

### 6. Documents to Upload
| Document | Format | Size Min | Size Max |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Photograph** | JPG/JPEG | 15 KB | 50 KB |
| **Signature** | JPG/JPEG | 5 KB | 20 KB |
| **Identity Proof** | PDF | 15 KB | 250 KB |

**Note:** Category/Domicile certificates are often verified during the "CAP" (Centralized Admission Process) counseling rounds, but basic details must be declared in the application form.

## Special Notes
- **Photo Specs:** 3.5cm x 4.5cm, color photo, white background.
- **Signature Specs:** Black ink on white paper.
- **Identity Proof:** Aadhar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, Driving License, or Passport.
