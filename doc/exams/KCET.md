# KCET (Karnataka Common Entrance Test)

## Official Links & Dates (2026)
- **Official Website:** [https://cetonline.karnataka.gov.in/kea/](https://cetonline.karnataka.gov.in/kea/)
- **Registration Status:** **OPEN**
- **Registration Start:** January 17, 2026
- **Registration End:** February 17, 2026
- **Exam Dates:** April 23 - 24, 2026

## Overview
KCET is conducted by the Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA) for admission to Engineering, Pharmacy, Agriculture, and Veterinary courses in Karnataka.

## Data Requirements

### 1. Student Information
- **Student Name:** As per SSLC marks card.
- **Father's Name.**
- **Mother's Name.**
- **Date of Birth.**
- **Gender.**
- **Mobile Number.**
- **Email ID.**
- **Nationality.**

### 2. Academic Details
- **Qualifying Exam:** 2nd PUC / Class 12.
- **University/Board Name.**
- **School/College Name.**
- **Passing Year.**
- **SSLC/10th Registration Number.**

### 3. Reservation & Claims (Complex Part)
- **Claiming Seat as:** Karnataka Candidate / Non-Karnataka.
- **Eligibility Clause Code:** (Codes 'a' to 'o' define eligibility based on years of study in Karnataka).
- **Caste Category:** GM / SC / ST / Cat-1 / 2A / 2B / 3A / 3B.
- **Special Reservations:**
  - Rural Quota (studied 10 years in rural Karnataka).
  - Kannada Medium Quota (studied 10 years in Kannada medium).
  - NCC / Sports / Defence / Ex-Defence / Scouts & Guides.
  - PwD.
  - HK Region (Hyderabad-Karnataka 371J).
- **Income Certificate:** RD Number required for Fee exemptions/SNQ quota.
- **Caste Certificate:** RD Number required.

### 4. Documents to Upload

## Special Notes
- **Left Thumb Impression:** Unique mandatory requirement for KCET.
- **Document Verification:** Unlike other exams, KCET has a physical/online document verification phase *after* the exam where original Study Certificates (signed by BEO) are checked.
- **SATS ID:** Student Achievement Tracking System ID (for Karnataka board students) is often asked.
