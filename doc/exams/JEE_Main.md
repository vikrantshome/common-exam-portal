# JEE Main (Joint Entrance Examination - Main)

## Official Links & Dates (2026)
- **Official Website:** [https://jeemain.nta.ac.in/](https://jeemain.nta.ac.in/)
- **Session 1 (January):**
  - **Exams:** January 21 - 29, 2026 (Ongoing)
  - **Result:** By February 12, 2026
- **Session 2 (April):**
  - **Registration Start:** First week of February 2026
  - **Registration End:** First week of March 2026
  - **Exam Dates:** April 2 - 9, 2026

## Overview
JEE Main is the standardized computer-based test for admission to various technical undergraduate programs in engineering, architecture, and planning across India.

## Data Requirements

### 1. Personal Details
- **Candidate Name:** Must match Class 10 certificate exactly.
- **Father's Name:** As per Class 10 records.
- **Mother's Name:** As per Class 10 records.
- **Date of Birth:** DD/MM/YYYY format.
- **Gender:** Male/Female/Third Gender.
- **Identity Type:** Aadhar Card (preferred), Passport, Voter ID, etc.
- **Identity Number:** Corresponding number.
- **State of Eligibility:** State from where Class 12 was passed/appearing.
- **Category:** General / Gen-EWS / OBC-NCL / SC / ST.
- **PwD Status:** Yes/No (If yes, requires certificate).
- **Diabetic:** Yes/No (for taking food items inside).
- **Place of Residence:** Urban/Rural.
- **Mode of Preparation:** Self-study, Coaching, etc.
- **Medium of Schooling:** English, Hindi, etc.

### 2. Contact Details
- **Mobile Number:** Candidate's & Parent's (for OTPs).
- **Email Address:** Primary & Secondary.
- **Permanent Address:** Full address with PIN Code.
- **Present Address:** Full address with PIN Code (if different).

### 3. Academic Details (Class 10)
- **Pass Status:** Passed.
- **Year of Passing:** YYYY.
- **Qualifying Exam:** 10th or Equivalent.
- **Place of Schooling:** Urban/Rural.
- **Type of School/College:** Govt, Private, KV, JNV, etc.
- **Qualifying Examination State:** State name.
- **Qualifying Examination District:** District name.
- **School Board:** CBSE, ICSE, State Board, etc.
- **Result Mode:** Percentage / CGPA.
- **Roll Number:** As per Class 10 mark sheet.
- **School Name & Address:** Full text.
- **School PIN Code:** 6-digit code.

### 4. Academic Details (Class 12)
- **Pass Status:** Passed / Appearing.
- **Year of Passing/Appearing:** 2024 / 2025.
- **Qualifying Exam:** Class 12 or Equivalent.
- **Place of Schooling:** Urban/Rural.
- **Type of School/College:** Govt, Private, etc.
- **Qualifying Examination State:** State name.
- **Qualifying Examination District:** District name.
- **School Board:** Board name.
- **Roll Number:** If allotted (for appearing candidates, might not be mandatory initially).
- **School Name & Address:** Full text.
- **School PIN Code:** 6-digit code.

### 5. Exam Centre Preferences
- **Choice 1-4:** Select State and City combinations.

## Special Notes
- **Photo Specs:** 80% face coverage, ears visible, white background. Spectacles allowed if used regularly. No caps/goggles.
- **Signature Specs:** Black ink on white paper usually preferred.
