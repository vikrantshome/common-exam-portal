# VITEEE (VIT Engineering Entrance Examination)

## Official Links & Dates (2026)
- **Official Website:** [https://viteee.vit.ac.in/](https://viteee.vit.ac.in/)
- **Registration Status:** **OPEN**
- **Registration End:** March 31, 2026
- **Exam Dates:** April 28 - May 3, 2026

## Overview
VITEEE is conducted by the Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) for admission to B.Tech programs at its Vellore, Chennai, AP, and Bhopal campuses.

## Data Requirements

### 1. New User Registration
- Name of the Candidate.
- Date of Birth.
- Gender.
- Nationality (Indian / NRI / PIO / OCI).
- Mobile Number.
- Email ID.

### 2. Application Form Details
- **Personal Details:**
  - Religion.
  - Community (Category).
  - Native State.
  - Parent/Guardian Name & Mobile Number.
  - Address for Correspondence.
- **Educational Details:**
  - Qualifying Exam: HSC / CBSE / ISCE / Others.
  - Exam Status: Passed / Appearing.
  - School Name.
  - School Address.
  - Year of Passing.
  - **Subject Combination:** PCME / PCBE (Maths vs Biology determines eligible branches).

### 3. Exam Center Preferences
- Select Test City (Choice 1, 2, 3).
