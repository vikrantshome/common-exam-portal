# CUET UG (Common University Entrance Test - Undergraduate)

## Official Links & Dates (2026)
- **Official Website:** [https://cuet.nta.nic.in/](https://cuet.nta.nic.in/)
- **Registration Status:** **OPEN (CLOSING SOON)**
- **Registration Start:** January 3, 2026
- **Registration End:** January 30, 2026
- **Exam Dates:** May 11 - 31, 2026

## Overview
CUET UG is the single window test for admission to Central Universities (DU, BHU, JNU, etc.) and many other participating universities.

## Data Requirements

### 1. Personal Details
- Candidate's Name, Father's/Mother's Name, Guardian's Name.
- DOB, Gender, Nationality, Identity Type.
- Category (Gen/OBC-NCL/SC/ST/EWS).
- **Kashmiri Migrant:** Yes/No.
- **Ward of Defence Personnel:** Yes/No.
- **Claim for NCC Quota:** Yes/No.
- **Claim for Sports Quota:** Yes/No.

### 2. Academic Details
- **Class 10 & 12:** Result Status, Year, Place of Schooling, Type of School, Country, State, District, Board, School Name, PIN Code, Roll No.

### 3. University / Programme Selection (Complex)
- User must select University Name -> Select Programme Name.
- (Can select multiple, crucial for eligibility).

### 4. Test Paper / Subject Selection (Complex)
- **Language:** (e.g., English, Hindi).
- **Domain Specific Subjects:** (e.g., Physics, Chem, Maths, CS).
- **General Test:** Yes/No.
- *Note:* Students can choose up to 6 subjects (previously 10).

### 5. Exam Center
- Choice of 4 cities.

## Special Notes
- **Subject Mapping:** The most critical part. A student applying for B.Sc Physics at DU needs specific subjects (PCM) compared to B.A. History. The form must handle this logic.
